Connect Google Trends

Launchly forecasts demand with real math (a least squares trend fit). Point it at a live Google Trends feed and the forecast runs on real search interest.

A tiny backend that returns monthly interest as JSON, for example a pytrends service. It receives ?kw=keyword and returns an array of 12 numbers.
Full honesty: Google Trends has no official public API, and browsers block a direct call from a local page (CORS). So a real live feed needs that small backend, or your Streamlit app can call pytrends server side. Leave the field empty and Launchly simulates the series, but the prediction math is real either way.
